It also facilitates the integration of metadata and external lists, enabling more systematic comparisons, easier querying, and better long-term preservation and reuse of the encoded data

### Three advantages of a custom schema:

1. A custom schema allows the creation of an artwork tag, containing structured data tailored specifically for pictorial works.

2. It enables the inclusion of categories documenting technical procedures, such as the material restoration and conservation of artworks.

3. It allows the recording of temporal or chronological data, which can help track the degradation or condition changes of the artworks during transportation or over time.
